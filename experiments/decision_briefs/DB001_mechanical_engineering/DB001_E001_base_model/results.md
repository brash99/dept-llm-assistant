# Experimental Results

## Primary Findings

The Decision Brief identified evidence supporting the following conclusions:

- A Mechanical Engineering major is closely linked to the Science and Engineering Research Center.
- Successful implementation requires a dedicated Makerspace / machine shop supervisor.
- Additional laboratory staffing is required.
- Significant recurring program funding has already been proposed.
- Existing planning documents strongly support development of the program.

---

## Missing Evidence

The Decision Brief identified several important gaps.

- Detailed faculty capacity.
- Laboratory equipment inventory.
- Recruitment projections.
- Detailed ABET implementation strategy.

---

## Architectural Lessons

The experiment demonstrated that Decision Brief generation is a richer problem than traditional Retrieval-Augmented Generation.

Rather than retrieving a single answer, the system successfully synthesized evidence, identified uncertainty, and highlighted missing institutional knowledge.

These observations will guide subsequent development of the Decision Brief architecture.
